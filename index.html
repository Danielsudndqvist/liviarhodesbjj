About Lívia

My name is Lívia Rhodes, and I'm 23 years old from Brazil, currently living in Sweden. Since the age of twelve, I have been training Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. For the last five years, my main focus in life has been becoming as good as possible and making the sport my full-time job by competing and teaching. I'm currently a brown belt, which is the last one before black belt. My biggest dream is to become a world champion in black belt, and I'm currently working hard on getting there, working, studying, and training at the same time.

My life in Brazil

I grew up in a town called Manhuaçu in the state of Minas Gerais. It's a smaller city about eight hours by car from Rio de Janeiro. When I started chasing my Jiu Jitsu career, I moved to another city called Vitória and started training at GF Team, which is one of the best teams in Brazil. I was competing a lot and made a lot of improvements with my new team.

My life in Sweden

Year 2022, I got an opportunity to go to Sweden to help out teaching some classes at an academy for three months. I accepted without thinking about it as I love traveling and always wanted to visit Europe. After a couple of weeks, I started dating my current boyfriend, and we started to apply for me to stay in Sweden. After a long process and a lot of traveling in and out of Sweden, I finally got approved in the summer of 2023. I dove straight into language courses, and I'm speaking Swedish without hindrance already. My club, where I train, is very supportive and does a lot to help me become a full-time athlete.
