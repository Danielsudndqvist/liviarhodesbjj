About Lívia

My name is Lívia Rhodes and im 23 years old from Brazil and currently living in Sweden. Since the age of twelve i have been training Brazilian Jui Jitsu, the last five years my main focus in life have been becoming as good as possible and makeing the sport my fulltime job by competeing and teaching. Im currently a brownbelt witch is the last one before blackbelt.

My life in Brazil

I grew up in a town called Manhuaçu in the state Minas Gerais. It's a smaller city about eight hours with car from Rio de Janeiro. When i srtated chasing my Jiu Jitsu career i moved to another city called Vitoria and started training at GF Team wich is one of the best teams in Brazil, i was competeing alot and made alot of improvements with my new team.
